Technical Excellence

The 5-Step Verification Protocol

01

Title Deed Audit

Thorough historical tracing of ownership through the 'Malpot' records to ensure zero litigation risk.

02

Physical Boundary Survey

On-site measurement using digital surveying tools to confirm Ropani-Aana-Paisa-Dam accuracy.

03

Road Access Certification

Categorization of access (Blacktopped vs Graveled) and official width verification in feet.

04

Zoning & Compliance

Checking municipal guidelines to ensure the property meets all building and land-use codes.

05

BS Date Alignment

Synchronization of all documentation with the Bikram Sambat calendar for local legal validity.
